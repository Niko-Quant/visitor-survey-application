Ordering

For standard cakes, we need at least 48 hours' notice. For custom and tiered cakes (weddings, birthdays, corporate), please allow two weeks. We deliver anywhere in Merseyside or Warrington for a flat fee of £5, or you can collect from the shop for free.

Before you order, have a look at our cake range and the customisation options.
