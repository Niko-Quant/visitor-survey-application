Customise Your Cake

Every cake we make can be customised. Pick your sponge, your icing, your toppings, and tell us about any dietary needs. Here's everything you can change.

🎂 Sponge Flavours

Choose the base of your cake:

  • Classic Vanilla
  • Rich Chocolate
  • Lemon Zest
  • Carrot & Walnut
  • Red Velvet
  • Coffee Espresso
  • Almond Frangipane

🍓 Fillings

What goes between the layers:

  • Strawberry jam
  • Raspberry jam
  • Lemon curd
  • Chocolate ganache
  • Whipped cream
  • Salted caramel
  • Fresh fruit compote

🎨 Icings

How your cake is finished on the outside:

  • Vanilla buttercream
  • Chocolate buttercream
  • Cream cheese frosting
  • Royal icing (hard set)
  • Fondant (smooth finish)
  • Naked / semi-naked
  • Drip cake effect

✨ Toppings & Decorations

The finishing touches:

  • Fresh berries
  • Edible flowers
  • Chocolate shavings
  • Sugar sprinkles
  • Piped buttercream roses
  • Edible gold leaf
  • Personalised message

🌱 Dietary Alternatives

We cater for most dietary needs. Please tell us in advance:

  • Gluten-free sponge
  • Vegan (egg & dairy free)
  • Dairy-free
  • Nut-free
  • Reduced sugar

📏 Size Options

Pick the size that suits your occasion:

  • 6" round - serves 6-8
  • 8" round - serves 10-14 (standard)
  • 10" round - serves 16-20
  • Two-tier - serves 30+
  • Cupcakes - pack of 12 or 24

Ready to Order?

Choose your options above, then get in touch to place your order. We'll need at least 48 hours' notice for standard cakes, and two weeks for wedding cakes.
